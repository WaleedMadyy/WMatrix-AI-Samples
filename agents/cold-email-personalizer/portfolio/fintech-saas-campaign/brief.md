# Portfolio Project Brief: Fintech SaaS Cold Email Campaign

## Client Profile

**Company:** Paystream
**Product:** Cloud-based accounts payable automation platform
**Stage:** Series A (just raised $12M)
**Team Size:** 40 employees
**HQ:** San Francisco, CA

## What Paystream Does

Paystream automates the entire accounts payable workflow for mid-market companies. Their platform handles invoice ingestion (email, PDF, EDI), AI-powered 3-way matching (invoice to PO to receipt), automated approval routing, and payment execution. The core differentiator is their matching engine — it uses machine learning trained on 2M+ invoices to auto-match line items, flag discrepancies, and learn from AP clerk corrections over time.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Weakness |
|---|---|
| Bill.com | Built for SMB, struggles with mid-market complexity (multi-entity, multi-currency). Matching is rules-based, not AI. |
| Tipalti | Strong on payment execution but weak on invoice processing. Expensive — $399/mo base + per-supplier fees. Implementation takes 3-6 months. |
| SAP Concur | Enterprise-grade. Overkill and overpriced for 200-1000 employee companies. 9-12 month implementation. |

## Key Differentiator

70% faster invoice processing versus legacy AP workflows. The AI-powered matching engine auto-matches 85%+ of invoices without human intervention, compared to industry average of 40-50% with rules-based systems.

## Case Study

**Client:** Unnamed logistics company (NDA — can reference industry and size)
- 300 employees, 4 warehouses
- Processing 1,500 invoices/month across 200+ vendors
- **Before Paystream:** 14-day average invoice processing time, 3 full-time AP clerks, frequent duplicate payments, 4% early payment discount capture rate
- **After Paystream:** 4-day average processing time, reduced AP team to 1.5 FTEs, eliminated duplicate payments, 38% early payment discount capture rate
- **Bottom line:** $180K/year savings in AP labor costs + $95K in captured early payment discounts
- **Implementation:** 6 weeks from kickoff to full production

## Campaign Objective

Generate qualified demo requests from VP of Finance / CFO-level prospects at mid-market companies (200-1000 employees) who are likely still running manual or semi-manual AP processes.

## Campaign Parameters

- **Sequence Length:** 4 emails
- **Sending Cadence:** Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
- **Target Persona:** VP of Finance, CFO, Controller, Director of Finance
- **Target Company Size:** 200-1000 employees
- **Target Industries:** Logistics, manufacturing, distribution, wholesale — high invoice volume industries
- **Goal:** Book a 20-minute demo call

## Constraints

- All emails must follow the Cold Email Personalizer's rules (50-90 words, 6-word max subject lines in lowercase, never start with "I", single low-commitment CTA)
- Each follow-up must introduce a new angle or value — no "just bumping this up"
- Tone: peer-to-peer, respectful, zero hype
- No misleading claims or fake personalization
