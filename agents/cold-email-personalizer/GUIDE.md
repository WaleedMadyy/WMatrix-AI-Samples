# Cold Email Personalizer Pro — Quick Start

> **You'll have a complete cold email campaign in 60 seconds. Here's how.**

---

## Step 1: Open Your AI Platform

Pick one and go there now:

| Platform | URL | Where to Find System Prompt |
|----------|-----|----------------------------|
| **Claude** (recommended) | [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) | Click **"Projects"** in the left sidebar → **"Create Project"** → scroll to **"Custom Instructions"** |
| **ChatGPT** | [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) | Click your **profile icon** (bottom-left) → **"Customize ChatGPT"** → paste in the **"Custom Instructions"** box |
| **Gemini** | [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com) | Click **"Settings"** (gear icon) → **"Extensions"** or use **"Gems"** → paste as system instructions |

---

## Step 2: Paste the Agent

1. Open the file called **`universal/system-prompt.md`** from this package
2. **Select all** the text inside it (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A)
3. **Copy** it (Ctrl+C / Cmd+C)
4. **Paste** it into the system prompt / custom instructions field from Step 1
5. **Save** or close the settings

```
✅ Checkpoint: You should see your custom instructions saved.
   The agent is now loaded. Start a new conversation.
```

---

## Step 3: Try It Now — Copy and Paste This

Start a new conversation and paste this example input:

```
Prospect: Lisa Chen, VP of Marketing at Datawise (B2B analytics startup, ~120 employees)
They just launched a new enterprise tier and are hiring 3 SDRs.

I sell: ReplyEngine — AI-powered email deliverability platform that helps sales teams
land in primary inbox instead of spam. We increased Salesloft's reply rates by 34%.

My name: Jordan

Tone: Direct
```
